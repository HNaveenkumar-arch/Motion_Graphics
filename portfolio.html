The Challenge

Revamping a decade-old legacy brand to appeal to modern Gen-Z audiences without alienating their core B2B enterprise clients. The visual language was entirely outdated, lacking dynamic digital presence, motion guidelines, and cross-platform consistency.

Our Solution

We engineered a highly dynamic, motion-first brand system. By utilizing immersive 3D web technologies, cinematic video elements, and a vibrant color palette, we bridged the gap between professional corporate trust and modern visual aesthetics.

The Process

Product Launch

Aura: Cinematic 3D Reveal

The Challenge

The client was launching a revolutionary smart device but only had CAD files and engineering blueprints. They needed a photorealistic, jaw-dropping promotional video for their global keynote event, and physical prototypes were not yet available.

Our Solution

We took their raw CAD models and rebuilt them from the ground up in Cinema4D. We applied hyper-realistic textures, complex lighting setups, and simulated fluid dynamics to create a completely CGI-driven product film that looked better than real life.

The Process

  • 01. CAD Optimization & Retopology
  • 02. Texturing & Look Development
  • 03. Camera Animation & Dynamics
  • 04. High-Fidelity Rendering & Compositing

The Results

The launch video garnered over 2 Million views in 48 hours, driving pre-orders up by 150%. The hyper-realistic renders were later repurposed for their entire global billboard campaign.
